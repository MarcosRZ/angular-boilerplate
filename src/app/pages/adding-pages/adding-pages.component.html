Adding Pages to this project

As you can see, adding new Pages to this boilerplate is very simple. Just use Angular CLI to create the files:

ng g component pages/MyAwesomeNewPage -m pages

And add a route in src/routes.ts, to reach it:

{{ '{' }} path: 'osom', component: MyAwesomeNewPageComponent {{ '},' }}

That's it, you can navigate to localhost/osom and see your new page
